      en: "The tool automatically collects data from two sources with completely different HTML structures, the Lega Basket Serie A website and the Euroleague website: roster, coach, player season statistics, and recent match results across both competitions. Matches are merged into a single timeline ordered by date, with the result computed automatically by comparing scores. Through an interactive interface, the coach selects the opponent team and players to build the depth chart; the tool then generates a complete PDF, including logo, roster, recent matches, depth chart, and per-player statistics, ready to consult before the game."

Le partite vengono unite in un'unica cronologia ordinata per data, con il risultato calcolato automaticamente dal confronto dei punteggi. Attraverso un'interfaccia interattiva, il coach seleziona la squadra avversaria e i giocatori per costruire il depth chart; lo strumento genera quindi un PDF completo — logo, roster, ultime partite, depth chart e statistiche per giocatore — pronto da consultare prima della partita."
